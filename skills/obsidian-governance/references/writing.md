# Writing Guidance

## Note Length

Use a **long note** when:
- Related ideas need to stay together for context or narrative
- The note documents a process with history or rationale

Use a **shorter note** when:
- The concept stands alone
- It will be linked from many places
- Reuse > narrative continuity

## Heading Structure

Use headings for any note longer than a quick fragment.
Prefer scan-friendly structure over wall-of-text prose.

## Atlas Notes

`_Atlas/Home.md` is the main control panel. Keep it useful with sections like:
- Now / Active Projects
- Areas
- Resources Pipeline
- System

Area notes should work as local entry points:
- What this area is for
- Related projects
- Important notes in this area
- Likely future topics

## Behavior Guardrails

- Do not rewrite daily notes aggressively
- Do not flatten project history unless requested
- Do not convert all notes into atomic notes
- Do not assume every note should become public/portfolio content
- When uncertain → ask a short question or mark the note for review
